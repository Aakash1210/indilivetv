What does Malang Do?

Malang is an Indian video-sharing social networking service owned by Indilivetv Entertainments company founded in 2020 by Tushar Verma & Tanuj Joshi. It is used to create short dance, lip-sync, comedy and talent videos. for Android in most market of India however, it only became available all-over INDIA. The application allows users to create short music and lipsync videos of 3 to 15 seconds, and short looping videos of 3 to 60 seconds.

Feature & Trends

The Malang mobile app allows users to create a short video of themselves which often feature music in the background, can be sped up, slowed down or edited with a filter. They can also add their own sound on top of the background music. To create a music video with the app, users can choose background music from a wide variety of music genres, edit with a filter and record a 15- second video with speed adjustments before uploading it to share with others on Indi-Shot’s or other social platforms. They can also film short lip-sync videos to popular songs.

The app's "react" feature allows users to film their reaction to a specific video, over which it is placed in a small window that is movable around the screen. Its "duet" feature allows users to film a video aside another video. The “duet” feature was another trademark of Malang Videos that users do not want to post yet can be stored in their "drafts". The user is allowed to see their "drafts" and post when they find it fitting.

The app allows users to set their accounts as "private." When first downloading the app, the user's account is public by default. The user can change to private in their settings. Private content remains visible to Malang, but is blocked from Malang users who the account holder has not authorized to view their content. Users can choose whether any other user, or only their "friends", may interact with them through the app via comments, messages, or "react" or "duet" videos. Users also can set specific videos to either “public”, “friends only”, or “private” regardless if the account is private or not. Users are also allowed to report accounts depending on the account's content either being spam or inappropriate. In Malang support center under "For Parents", they reassure the parents that inappropriate content for their children can be blocked and reported.

The “Follow me” page on Malang is a feed of videos that are recommended to users based on their activity on the app. Content is generated by Malang artificial intelligence (AI) depending on what kind of content a user liked, interacted with, or searched. Users can also choose to add to favorites or select "not interested" on videos in their for you page. Malang combines the user's enjoyed content to provide videos that they would also enjoy. Users and their content can only be featured on the “follow me” page if they are 14 or over as per Malang policy. Users under 14 will not show up under the “follow me” page, the sounds page, or under any hashtags. When users follow other users, a "following" page is located on the left of the "follow me" page. This is a page to only see the videos from the accounts a user follows. Users can also add videos, hashtags, filters, and sounds to their “saved” section. When creating a video, they can refer to their saved section, or create a video straight from it.

  • What is the purpose of Malang?

    Malang is a social media platform for creating, sharing and discovering short music videos, think Karaoke for the digital age. Malang will be using by young people as an outlet to express themselves through singing, dancing, comedy, and lip-syncing

  • Easy Content Creation, Sharing, and Viewing

    The Malang app has simplified video creation and sharing and taken it to the next level. All users have to do is record anything and everything from their daily routines and post it instantly. Due to the short format, neither the video-creation nor the watching process takes much time or effort. Additionally, this short-form video content is played as soon as a user opens the app. The videos start playing one-by-one and a viewer gets lost in a sea of fun, entertaining, addictive video content. Due to this addictive nature of the type of content, it is very easy for people to keep watching random videos for hours.

  • Artificial intelligence

    Malang employs artificial intelligence to analyze users' interests and preferences through their interactions with the content, and display a personalized content feed for each user. Malang has an algorithm in which they process the user's preferences based on the videos they "like", comment on, and also how long they watch the video. Compared to other consumer algorithms such as YouTube and Netflix Or Zee5 with a list of recommended videos,Malang interprets the user's individual preferences and provides content that they would enjoy.

  • How Can Brands Leverage Malang?

    Malang does not have any space for traditional display ads and is not even in competition with other social media platforms, in terms of it being a marketing channel. However, due to its fast growth and rising popularity, many brands are now realising the potential of Malang as a marketing channel. Another way in which brands can use Malang is to collaborate with Indi-Shot’s celebrities to create brand-specific promotional content. This will be similar to any other influencer collaboration, just with the 15-second Indi-Shot’s video format

  • Conclusion

    In a nutshell, Malang is a fun, entertaining, and addictive app which has seen a surge in popularity in the last few months. The Malang app also has the potential to become the next big marketing and social networking platform. However, how the app creators leverage this potential and maintain the current popularity of the app, has yet to be seen. What type of Malang content are you addicted to?
